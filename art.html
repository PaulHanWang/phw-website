Unreal Engine Renders

Created in Unreal Engine 5.5 using 3D files of the real SISU 26E design, these renders were made using the real CAD files of the new concept class vehicle by Edinburgh University Formula Student. They were used for the official posters of the team in the Formula Student UK design competition, featuring special flax fibre bodypanels and an unpolished, raw material look. They were also later used as the base of the design of the EUFS posters.
