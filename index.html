Official Website of Paul Han Wang.

Vehicle dynamics engineer, sailing instructor, and FAA licensed pilot.



PROJECTS:

EDINBURGH UNIVERSITY FORMULA STUDENT

At Edinburgh University Formula Student, I help design and build an open-wheel racecar in a fully student led team, and compete in international competitions that take my engineering skills to the next level.

MINNEAPOLIS SAILING CENTER

At the Minneapolis Sailing Center, I teach passionate students of all ages, of all skill levels. From complete beginners, to lifelong sailors, to advanced racers, I teach people from all walks of live how to improve their sailing skills, all while improving my own teaching and leadership skills.

TWIN CITIES FLIGHT

At Twin Cities Flight, I took my learning to new neights (literally). By combining technical aeronautical knowledge, aviation regulations and legislation, and precise vehicle control in one of the most high-stakes, high-pressure environments on the planet, I reinforced my confidence, communication, and leadership skills.

CINEMATOGRAPHY & PHOTOGRAPHY

7 films, over 10 years, across 4 continents; I taught myself the ins and outs of cinematography. From home movies and road trip videos to wildlife photography and cinematic short films, my creative eye has given me a strong understanding of visual storytelling and the communication of information.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

Using 3D and 2D modeling software, I created personal marketing material and official marketing material for Edinburgh University Formula Student. Whether it's Unreal Engine, Unity Game Engine, Canva, or just plain Microsoft Paint, I have loved going above and beyond making fun art projects for myself and others.
